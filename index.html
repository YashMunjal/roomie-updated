About us

FinnRooms solves the problem of Paying Guest service in Chandigarh for students. Usually PGs don’t serve food and laundry service up to marks for students. Even if they are providing these services, students aren’t able to find them easily. We are bringing PGs and students on a common platform to solve this problem.

Students from distant places come to Chandigarh to prepare prestigious engineering or medical college entrance examination. The basic necessity of a human includes a roof, food and laundry service. At FinnRooms we are trying to solve these problems to improve the living condition for those students.
